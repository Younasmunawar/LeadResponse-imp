Built for real conversations
Unclear answer

Kenny plays alternating clarification prompts and listens again.

“Please repeat”

The current question is replayed only when the caller asks for it.

Accessible voice + typing

Every question accepts speech and text. A caller who cannot speak, has a speech difficulty, is in a noisy place, or simply prefers typing can complete the same qualification flow.

Typed replies take priority

The microphone listens for up to seven seconds, but submitting text immediately stops listening and processes the typed answer first.

No speech

If no voice is detected, the caller can type an answer or ask the system to listen again without losing progress.

End call

Audio and listening stop immediately, available answers are saved, and the call screen closes automatically.
