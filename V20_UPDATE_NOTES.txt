Kenny Original Voice Agent v20

Live validation changes
- Local validation remains first and now has a much larger phrase directory.
- Only two Gemini validation keys are used.
- Each validation key has a 3.5-second hard timeout.
- Timeout/network/invalid-response errors may move to the second key.
- HTTP 429 quota errors stop the Gemini validation chain immediately.
- When Gemini validation cannot be used, strict local fallback is returned without showing a provider error to the caller.
- Final analysis continues to use the two dedicated finalization keys.

Local validation enhancements
- More consent, refusal, busy, cautious-consent, and repeat-question phrases.
- More purchase, lease, personal, family, business, and investment variants.
- Expanded Abu Dhabi area aliases and property types.
- More numeric, ranged, and spoken budget formats.
- Existing intent is used to assess whether a budget is positive, neutral, or negative.
- More timeline phrases and common speech-recognition variants.
- More payment, WhatsApp, alternate-contact, and follow-up-time phrases.
- More hard-negative and clearly unrelated phrases.
